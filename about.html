About Jenni

Hi, my name is Jenni!

I'm a self taught web developer. I run a blog with friends called Southern Friends & Co. We love to cook and share our recipes. As you can probably tell from the title, I'm from the south. That means good cookin'! Grandma had me in the kitchen since I was four, and I really haven't left it. My favorite foods to make are savory dinners and full course breakfasts.

My loves in life are cooking, traveling, and family! Cooking can be done anywhere you can light a fire. And don't let me get started on where I want to travel! Family is everything. My partner, Cody, and I have two wonderful fur-babies: Coco and Chewy. Both are Yorkie rescues and made us fall in love within the first moment of meeting them! They definitely run the house.
